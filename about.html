Mang Inasal Malolos

Welcome to Mang Inasal, we serve the nation's best-tasting chicken inasal with unli-rice.

For more than 20 years, Mang Inasal, the Philippines' Grill Expert, has been providing amazing Ihaw-Sarap food and Unli-Saya experiences. In 2003, at the age of 26, our founder Edgar “Injap” Sia II built the first branch in his hometown of Iloilo City. Additionally, he selected a brand name that honors all of the grill experts who have mastered the technique of preparing the most well-known cuisine in Iloilo.

Our Malolos location combines the vibrant Mang Inasal experience with the rich cultural heritage of this historic city, creating a dining experience that's uniquely ours.


How to Get Here?

Located near Minor Basilica and Cathedral Parish of the Immaculate Conception, accessible by jeepney or tricycle from anywhere in Malolos.
