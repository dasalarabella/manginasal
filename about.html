Mang Inasal Malolos

Welcome to our Malolos branch, where we bring the authentic flavors of Filipino grilled chicken to the historic city of Malolos, Bulacan.

Since opening our doors, we've been proud to serve the community with our signature Ihaw-Sarap meals, unlimited rice, and warm Filipino hospitality.

Our Malolos location combines the vibrant Mang Inasal experience with the rich cultural heritage of this historic city, creating a dining experience that's uniquely ours.
